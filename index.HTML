A Timeline of Innovation & Recognition

October 20, 2022

The Ministry of Health evaluates and provides a letter of support for the initial "Nurse Call System," recognizing its potential service to the health sector.

April 4, 2023

The Addis Ababa City Administration Health Bureau issues a letter requesting cooperation and support for the system's implementation.

July 19, 2023

The "Nurse Calling System" is officially registered and receives a Copyright & Neighboring Rights Registration Certificate from the Ethiopian Intellectual Property Authority.

April 24, 2025

The Ministry of Innovation and Technology formally endorses the full ISHCS project, confirming its alignment with national healthcare technology priorities.

May 9, 2025

The Ministry of Health requests support from the World Health Organization (WHO) for the pilot testing phase of the innovative system.

July 15, 2025

Successfully completes the "AI and Career Empowerment" course from the University of Maryland, further strengthening the project's AI foundations.
